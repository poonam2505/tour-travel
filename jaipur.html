About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famous for its pink-colored buildings, royal palaces, and vibrant culture. It's a perfect destination for those seeking history, art, and architectural marvels.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Jaipur is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and perfect for exploring forts and palaces.

How to Reach Jaipur

  • By Air: Jaipur International Airport connects the city to major domestic and international destinations.
  • By Train: Jaipur Junction is a key railway station with trains from all over India.
  • By Road: Jaipur is well connected by national highways, especially from Delhi and Agra.

How to Explore Jaipur

Explore Jaipur by hiring a cab, taking a rickshaw, or using local buses. Heritage walks and guided tours offer deeper insights into the city's royal past.
