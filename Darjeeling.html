About Darjeeling

Darjeeling, nestled in the Himalayan foothills of West Bengal, is renowned for its world-famous tea, panoramic views of Mt. Kanchenjunga, colonial charm, and the scenic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.

Things to Do in Darjeeling

Tiger Hill: Witness a magical sunrise over the Himalayas, with views of Mt. Everest and Kanchenjunga.

Darjeeling Toy Train: Ride the UNESCO-listed Himalayan Railway for a nostalgic and scenic journey.

Tea Gardens: Stroll through lush tea estates like Happy Valley and taste world-renowned Darjeeling tea.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Darjeeling is from March to May and October to December when the skies are clear and the weather is cool and refreshing.

How to Reach Darjeeling

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Bagdogra (about 70 km), connected to major Indian cities.
  • By Train: New Jalpaiguri (NJP) is the nearest major railway station, with toy train connections to Darjeeling.
  • By Road: Regular taxis and buses connect Darjeeling to Siliguri, Kalimpong, and Gangtok.

How to Explore Darjeeling

Explore Darjeeling by foot, local taxis, and toy trains. Guided nature walks and heritage tours are great ways to experience the culture and charm of the region.
